# Aria Vance

> **Status:** concept (2026-06-27)

## Concept

A disgraced steel-city engineer who reads the resonance of broken machines — she hears what a mechanism *wants* to do, and bends it.

## Appearance

Soot-grey coat over brass-laced gauntlets; one eye replaced by a humming resonance lens.

## Abilities

- **Resonance read** — senses the intended motion of any mechanism on contact.
- **Overtune** — temporarily pushes a machine past its rated limits (with a failure cost).

## Relationships

- **The Foundry Guild** — exiled from it; still bound by its debts.
- **Kael Drathmoor** — uneasy ally, opposite temperament.

## Arc

From bending machines for survival to trusting people — learns the cost of overtuning *herself*.
